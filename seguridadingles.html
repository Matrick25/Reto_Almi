Importance of Workplace Safety

In the field of computer science and robotics, workplace safety and health are very important due to the constant interaction between people and machines. Risks can be divided into several categories, each with its own hazards and solutions.

Physical Risks

Physical risks, such as noise, radiation, and extreme temperatures, are common. To protect against these risks, it is important to:

  • Use hearing protection.
  • Use radiation protection equipment.
  • Control the environment adequately, ensuring safe temperatures and conditions.

Chemical Risks and Contaminants

Chemical risks and air contaminants are also dangerous. Important measures include:

  • Use of personal protective equipment (PPE).
  • Ensure proper ventilation in work areas.

Biological Risks

In terms of biological risks, such as microorganisms and allergens, it is crucial to:

  • Manage the workload effectively.
  • Have clear policies against harassment and violence.

Risk Prevention in Robotics

To prevent occupational risks in computing, it is necessary to:

  • Identify and analyze hazards.
  • Implement effective control measures.
  • Continuously train employees.
